A different kind of learning

Learning that feels like childhood, and builds real life skills.

We believe children learn best through experience, creativity, movement and meaningful connection.

Our programme blends nature-based learning, cultural exploration, creative expression, practical life skills and time to play.

Children are supported, encouraged and given the space to discover who they are.

What children experience every day

🌿

Nature-Based Learning

Exploring nature, gardens, beaches and the local environment.

🎨

Creative Expression

Art, music, drama, craft and imaginative projects.

🌍

Culture & Local Life

Learning through Goan culture, traditions and community.

🔧

Life Skills

Cooking, gardening, teamwork and practical life experiences.

🏃

Movement & Play

Outdoor play, yoga, games and plenty of movement.

🤝

Connection & Community

Building friendships, kindness, confidence and belonging.

A rhythm that supports children to thrive

Morning
  • Circle time & intention setting
  • Nature walks & outdoor learning
  • Creative or project-based sessions
  • Snack & free play
Midday
  • Nutritious lunch together
  • Rest & quiet time
  • Reading, journaling or calm activities
  • Open-ended play
Afternoon
  • Workshops & hands-on projects
  • Cultural activities & explorations
  • Movement, games & connection
  • Reflection & closing circle

Small groups. Big care.

Our programme runs in small groups with a high adult-to-child ratio.

Our facilitators are experienced, background-checked and passionate about working with children.

Safety, respect and emotional wellbeing come first.
