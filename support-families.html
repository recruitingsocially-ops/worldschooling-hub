How we support families.

Your family's wellbeing and safety are our priority. We have created clear systems, caring staff and a supportive community so you can relax and enjoy this experience together.

Apply for 2027Download the Brochure

We believe families deserve a safe, supportive and well-held environment.

Our approach is built around four key pillars.

👨‍👩‍👧

Safeguarding & Child Safety

Clear expectations, child-first decision making and staff selected with care.

🏥

First Aid & Practical Support

First-aid trained staff and calm systems for everyday support.

❤️

Community Standards

Clear guidelines around communication, respect, conflict and shared living.

💬

Transparent Communication

Onboarding, expectations, policies and support before and during your stay.

Safety, care and wellbeing at the heart of everything we do.

👶

Child-centred approach

Children's wellbeing comes first in every decision we make.

🌟

Experienced team

Our team are carefully selected, background-checked and experienced in working with children and families.

🏥

Trained & prepared

First aid trained staff, safety procedures, emergency plans and regular reviews.

🛡️

Secure environment

Gated property, secure access, 24/7 onsite staff and a focus on safety.

🧠

Emotional wellbeing

We support children and teens emotionally, not just practically.

We create a space where children can be children, teens can be themselves and parents can breathe, knowing their family is in good hands.

Before, during and after your stay

01

Before you arrive

  • Personal onboarding call
  • Detailed welcome guide
  • Clear expectations and policies
  • Help with planning your trip
  • Any questions answered
  • Honest conversations
  • Preparation for a smooth arrival
02

During your stay

  • Onsite support from our team
  • Children's programme and Teen Hub
  • Regular check-ins
  • Ongoing communication
  • Practical help when you need it
  • A community that looks out for you
  • A place where you belong
03

After your stay

  • Continued connection
  • Feedback and reflections
  • Support with onward travel if needed
  • Special offers for returning families
  • You leave as family, not just guests
