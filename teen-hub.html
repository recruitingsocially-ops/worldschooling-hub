A space for teens to connect, grow and belong.

The Teen Hub is a dedicated space for 12–17 year olds to build real friendships, develop life skills, explore their interests and feel supported to be themselves.

More than just free time

Teens need their own space.

A place where they can connect beyond screens, be part of something meaningful and build skills for life.

Our Teen Hub gives teenagers the freedom to be themselves, try new things, take on challenges and be supported by a caring team who get them.

  • Make real friendships
  • Build confidence and independence
  • Explore passions and new skills
  • Be active, creative and outdoors
  • Be part of a global community
  • Have a voice and be heard
