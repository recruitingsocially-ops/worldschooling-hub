General questions

Worldschooling Hub Goa is a nature-based co-living community in South Goa, India, designed for worldschooling families. Families join four-week cohorts on a 3-acre tropical property where children have a nature-based learning programme, teenagers have their own community space, and parents can cowork, rest and connect.
The Hub is for families who are exploring alternative education or worldschooling, who work remotely, and who want their children to learn through real life and nature. Parents typically value community, wellbeing and intentional living.
We are a small, community-focused hub — not a large resort programme. Everything is designed around connection, wellbeing and real relationships. You leave as family, not just guests.
Accommodation, all meals and snacks, the full children's programme, Teen Hub, coworking space, wellbeing sessions, community events, excursions and 24/7 onsite support.
2027 cohorts run in January, February and March. See our Dates & Pricing page for exact start and end dates.
Complete our short application form on the Apply page. We'll be in touch within 48 hours to discuss your family's needs and confirm your place.

Children's programme

Mornings include circle time, nature walks and creative sessions. Midday is lunch and rest. Afternoons include workshops, cultural activities, movement and a closing circle.
The children's programme is designed for ages 4–11. The Teen Hub is for ages 12–17. Both run simultaneously so parents have time for themselves.
Through play, nature, creativity, movement and real-world experience. We are child-led, project-based and focused on curiosity rather than curriculum.
Yes. All facilitators are experienced, background-checked and passionate about working with children in nature-based environments.
We welcome all children. Our small group sizes mean every child is noticed and supported. Facilitators are trained in emotional wellbeing and create warm, inclusive environments.

Teen Hub

A dedicated community space for 12–17 year olds to connect, explore, create and grow alongside peers from around the world.
12–17 year olds. Teens are grouped roughly by age for some activities but mix freely during community time.
A mix of adventure, creative projects, life skills, beach time, group challenges, community events and free time. Activities are shaped with teen input each week.
Yes. Free time is built into every day. Teens have space to rest, explore independently and simply be themselves.
